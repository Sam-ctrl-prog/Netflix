About Me

As CEO of Instagram, I lead a global team dedicated to connecting over a billion people worldwide through photos, videos, and stories. Prior to heading Instagram, I spent 10 years at Facebook (now Meta) where I helped build News Feed and worked across product and design teams.

Our mission is to bring people closer to the people and things they love. Every product decision we make is guided by this north star.

I'm passionate about building products that are simple yet impactful, and creating experiences that serve communities worldwide. My focus is on leading Instagram through its next phase of growth while ensuring it remains a safe, positive place for self-expression and connection.
